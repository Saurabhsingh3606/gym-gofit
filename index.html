Top 5 Weight Loss Tips in Delhi

Struggling to shed those extra kilos? At our weight loss gym Delhi members follow these five proven strategies: start with a calorie-deficit diet tailored to Indian cuisine, combine HIIT with strength training three times a week, stay hydrated through Delhi's humid summers, track your macros with our in-house nutritionist, and maintain consistent sleep hygiene. GoFit's certified coaches have helped hundreds of Delhi residents transform with exactly this approach.

Beginners · Workout

Best Workout Plan for Beginners

New to the gym? As the leading beginner gym Delhi has to offer, GoFit recommends a simple 3-day-per-week full-body routine for your first month. Focus on compound movements — squats, push-ups, rows, and lunges — with lighter weights and perfect form. Our Starter Plan members receive a dedicated onboarding session where a certified trainer sets up your personalised program. Consistency beats intensity at this stage, so showing up regularly is the most important step you can take on your fitness journey.

By Arjun Sharma · Head Strength Coach · 6 min read
🧘
Wellness · Yoga

Why Yoga Belongs in Every Gym Routine

Many gym-goers in Delhi skip yoga thinking it won't add to their physique — but our members who combine strength training with weekly yoga sessions at GoFit report faster recovery, better posture, and significantly reduced injury risk. Yoga improves the joint mobility needed for heavier lifts, reduces cortisol (the stress hormone that stores belly fat), and builds the mental focus needed for peak performance. GoFit's RYT-500 certified instructor Priya Kapoor runs daily sessions open to all membership tiers.

By Priya Kapoor · Yoga & Wellness Coach · 4 min read

GoFit Gym memberships start at ₹1,299/month (Starter), ₹2,799/month (Pro), and ₹4,999/month (Elite). All plans come with no joining fees for the first month and are cancellable anytime.
Does GoFit Gym offer personal training in Delhi?+
Yes! All GoFit Gym Pro and Elite members receive dedicated personal training sessions (4–8 per month) with our certified coaches. You can also purchase standalone PT sessions from ₹799.
What are GoFit Gym's opening hours?+
GoFit Gym is open Monday–Friday from 5:00 AM to 11:00 PM, and on weekends (Saturday & Sunday) from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
Can beginners join GoFit Gym?+
Absolutely! GoFit Gym welcomes all fitness levels. Our Starter Plan is specifically designed for beginners, and you'll receive a free onboarding session with a trainer to get you started safely.
